Luhan Tang

Ph.D. Student @ University of California, Riverside

I am currently a second-year Ph.D. student at the University of California, Riverside working with Professors Greg Ver Steeg and Weixin Yao.

I received my B.S. in Statistics from Beijing Normal University, where I also took coursework in Economics at Peking University.

My research interests include generative models (e.g., diffusion models).

I am passionate about capturing the world through my lens. Welcome to visit my photo gallery.
